Save the Sea Turtles!

Mission Statement

Our organization is dedicated to raising awareness and inspiring action around sea turtle safety. Currently, only about 1 in 1,000 sea turtles live to be adults. Most threats to sea turtles are caused by humans, such as oil spills, marine debris entagelement, and development-caused habitat loss. We want to change this, and increase the number of surviving sea turtles. Join us in our journey towards a safer world for our sea turtle friends.

About Us

Take Action

Here is a list of things that you can do:

  • Turn off lights at night that are visible from the beach
  • Reduce the amount of garbage that you produce
  • Don't litter!
  • Host a beach clean-up
  • Be aware of & avoid sea turtle nesting areas
  • Reduce the amount of chemicals you use
  • Volunteer with us
  • Donate to our cause

Ideas for action taken from http://www.defenders.org/sites/default/files/publications/five-things-you-can-do-to-save-sea-turtles.pdf
